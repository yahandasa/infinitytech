Enhance the existing Infinity Tech website design to achieve a more premium, serious, and investor-ready feel. Focus on subtle depth, refined color usage, and high-quality micro-interactions.

---

## Visual Depth & Professional Feel

* Add subtle layered depth:

  * Use soft gradients in backgrounds (very low opacity)
  * Example: radial gradient from top (cyan tint at 3–5% opacity fading to dark)

* Introduce a slight “glass / blur” effect on navbar:

  * backdrop-blur with transparency
  * Gives modern high-end feel

* Add thin borders:

  * 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.05)
  * Avoid heavy shadows, rely more on contrast and layers

---

## Advanced Color Refinement

Refine the palette to feel more premium:

* Background base: #0A0E13 (slightly deeper than before)

* Add subtle gradient overlays:

  * #0A0E13 → #0F1720 (vertical gradient)

* Accent color upgrade:

  * Primary: #22D3EE
  * Accent hover: #06B6D4
  * Accent glow (VERY subtle): rgba(34,211,238,0.15)

* Add secondary accent (very limited use):

  * Soft blue: #3B82F6 (only for highlights or links)

⚠️ Keep usage controlled. No color noise.

---

## Micro-Interactions (IMPORTANT)

Add subtle, smooth, professional animations:

### Cards

* On hover:

  * Slight lift: translateY(-4px)
  * Slight scale: 1.01
  * Border becomes more visible
  * Add soft glow (very low opacity cyan)

### Buttons

* Smooth hover:

  * Background shift (cyan → darker cyan)
  * Add inner glow or light sweep effect

### Links

* Underline animation (slide in from left)

---

## Motion & Transitions

* Use consistent timing:

  * 0.2s – 0.3s ease-in-out

* Add:

  * Fade-in on scroll
  * Staggered animation for project cards

⚠️ No aggressive animations. Keep everything subtle and smooth.

---

## Hero Section Enhancement

* Add soft animated background:

  * Moving gradient OR
  * Very subtle particle/dots effect (low opacity)

* Title treatment:

  * Slight gradient text (cyan → white)
  * Or glow effect (very subtle)

---

## Typography Polish

* Increase contrast between headings and body
* Add letter-spacing for headings (slight)
* Use larger hero title (clear hierarchy)

---

## Premium Details (Key Differentiators)

* Add small technical UI touches:

  * Tags styled like “chips”
  * Status indicators (e.g. “Active”, “Completed”)
  * File-type icons (PCB / Code / 3D)

* Add section dividers:

  * subtle lines or gradient separators

---

## Lighting & Glow System

Use glow VERY carefully:

* Only on:

  * Active buttons
  * Hover states
  * Important highlights

* Glow example:

  * box-shadow: 0 0 20px rgba(34,211,238,0.15)

⚠️ Avoid overuse to maintain seriousness.
